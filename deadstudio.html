KeyBoard Warrior

Battle your keyboard for something better than twitter clout. In this arcade style RPG, written fully as a C++ console app, the goal is to survive as long as you can and aim for the highest score.
Combat through your keyboard with reaction speed and accuracy. Explore dungeons and a multiple endings storyline between rounds of monster slaying.

MP3 Audio Player

Play your favorite MP3s with this simple audio player. Written in C++ using the SDL2 library, this program allows you to play, pause, stop, and skip through your favorite MP3 playlist.
